Our Mission

Breaking Stereotype. Building dreams

Girls 4 STEM is a Malaysia-based non-profit organization advocating to close the gender gap in technology. By creating STEM-based experiences and opportunities for young girls to participate, we hope to inspire them to pursue a career in STEM and make positive impacts in our society.
