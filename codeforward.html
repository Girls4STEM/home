About the camp

#CodeForward is a 2-week virtual introductory computer science course for Malaysian students age 14 - 19. This 2-week virtual program will teach students the computer science skills they need to make an impact in thier community while preparing for a career in tech. Participants will get a chance to meet women in tech career and join a supportive and passionate group of students in tech. By the end of 2-week, students will have 4 weeks to complete a final project for presentation

The syllabus of #CodeForward is inspired by Harvard Computer Science Courses. Students will attend classes from 10 am - 12 pm, and a lab session to experience hands-on coding in a group from 2 pm - 4 pm on weekdays. No assignments are intended to be completed outside of class and lab time. Zad (a Harvard CS major) will be teaching the classes, with the help of teaching fellows for labs and debugging session.

Students are encouraged to apply as early as possible although the deadline is 30th April 2021. All applied students will scheduled a 15 minutes intro meeting with Zad / Celeste, just to talk about your interest in computer science and make sure your commitment. Depending on the number of applicants, selection might required.

A laptop/computer with audio and video capabilities, and internet access that is sufficient for zoom class. All activities and projects will be web-based and you'll need to have zoom for classes and labs, access emails and websites. No prior software or pre-existing program needs to be downloaded. We will teach everything from Scratch

100% free for all students.

Students are expected to be present during the camp (29 May - 13 Jun). Classes are not held during weekend. Students can be absent from classes with valid reasons for not more than 5 times. Class recording will be distributed for those unable to attend live classes. However, students are expected to attend all lab session.
