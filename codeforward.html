About #CodeForward

31 May - 13 June 2021

#Girls4STEM Malaysia will be hosting a virtual competitive coding summer program for high & middle school students (age 14 - 19) in Malaysia. This free two-week summer program for young individuals will teach young leaders to build real-life web - application. All backgrounds are welcome: whether you’ve never written a line of code or you’re a full-fledged hacker. This once in a lifetime opporturnity is for anyone who's passionate and interested in learning a new superpower. Don’t miss the opporturnity! APPLY by April 30!

For questions regarding the program, refer to QnA below or email us at girls4stemmy@gmail.com
